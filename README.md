## About Me

I build **enterprise-grade software** — scalable systems, clean APIs, and cloud-native architectures that organizations actually rely on.

My work spans the full stack: from designing microservice backends in **.NET Core with Clean Architecture** to shipping production frontends in **React, Next.js, and Angular**. I've led development teams under **Scrum methodology** — owning sprint planning, task distribution, retrospectives, and delivery — and I've worked solo on complex freelance projects when that's what the job demanded.

I've reverse-engineered legacy systems to extract business logic, identified architectural bottlenecks, and rebuilt solutions that are secure, decoupled, and maintainable. I design for scalability from day one, not as an afterthought.

Some of what I've shipped: an **airport billing platform with AI-based aircraft recognition** that automates invoice generation, a **real estate CRM** with omnichannel messaging and an AI chatbot, **asset management systems**, cloud backup validation services, and internal dashboards for government and private institutions — all with **AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines** in the mix.

> *I don't just write code. I architect systems, lead teams, and ship things that work.*
